# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

> haru (hereinafter the "Operator") publishes this Privacy Policy to protect members' personal information and to comply with applicable laws (the Personal Information Protection Act, the Network Act, etc.).

## Items of Personal Information Collected and Purpose of Use

The Operator collects and uses the following personal information to the extent necessary to provide the service, including member identification and authentication, matching, chat, and safe operation of the service.

- **Information provided by the member**: account information such as email address or social login identifier and password; profile information such as date of birth, gender, nationality, language, profile photo, and self-introduction; voice data such as voice clone, voice intro, and voice messages; and content such as messages the member creates or sends, together with their translation results.
- **Information generated during use of the service**: usage records, device and access information, notification tokens, and other information necessary for the operation, improvement, and safety of the service.

The collected information is used only within the scope of the purpose of collection and is destroyed upon account deletion or expiration of the retention period.

## Outsourcing of Processing to Third Parties and Cross-Border Transfer

For the operation, maintenance, improvement, and safety of the service, the Operator may outsource tasks such as database hosting, voice synthesis, automatic translation, and content moderation to external providers, and these providers may be located overseas, including in the United States. Members are deemed to have consented to such outsourcing and cross-border transfer upon sign-up.

## Processing of Voice Data

The Operator processes members' voice for the purposes of creating voice clones and synthesizing voice messages and voice intros. Voice data is retained only for the period necessary for the processing purpose, and a voice clone can be deleted by re-recording or by deleting the account. Synthesized audio files are destroyed after a certain period has elapsed, or when the member changes the relevant content or deletes their account.

## Destruction of Personal Information

The Operator destroys personal information without delay once the purpose of collection has been achieved or the retention period has expired. However, information that must be retained under applicable laws — such as for dispute resolution or cooperation with investigations — is stored separately from other information for the relevant period and then destroyed.

## Rights of the Data Subject and Protection of Personal Information

Members may at any time request access to, correction of, deletion of, or suspension of the processing of their personal information. Access and correction are available in the in-app profile, deletion is available through in-app account deletion, and other requests, inquiries regarding personal information processing, and appeals regarding suspended accounts may be submitted to the Personal Information Protection Officer (Sejin Lim, sejinim02@gmail.com).

The Operator takes technical and administrative measures to protect personal information, including encryption of communication channels, access control, and encrypted storage of passwords.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised in accordance with changes in laws or the service. In the event of any revision, members will be notified before the effective date.
